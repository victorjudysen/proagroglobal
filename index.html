About Pro Agro Global

A dynamic and leading company in the distribution of modern agriculture products, facilities and technologies with 9 years of vast experience

Our Story

Pro Agro Global Ltd was established with a purpose of supplying general purpose products and materials to major companies, SME's and organizations as well as supply and installation of modern agriculture products and facilities. In 9 years, the company has already gathered vast experience in its competitive edge to ensure customer satisfaction at the utmost level.

We have a team of highly trained professionals with vast experience to go beyond the sale to ensure that our clients are completely satisfied with our products and services.

Our Mission

To become the world-class supplier of products, facilities as well as technical solutions provider in East Africa, facilitating growth of modern agriculture.
