Company Overview

We are involved in design and building of center pivot systems, providing modern solutions that enhance water efficiency and increase agricultural productivity. Our systems are designed for large-scale farms offering automated and precise irrigation to help farmers conserve water while boosting crop yields.

With focus on durability and ease of maintenance, we deliver reliable solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern agriculture. Our team comprises proficient individuals with extensive technical skills and experience, committed to providing dedicated after-sales services and support to ensure the growth of our clients in modern farming practices.

What We Do

We aim to meet the needs and demands of our clients by exceeding their expectations

Center Pivot Systems

Advanced Water Management Solutions

We supply, design, and install center pivot systems that manage soil water levels efficiently. Water application efficiency can reach up to 80%, depending on management and installation design.

Our systems are designed for large-scale farms offering automated and precise irrigation to help farmers conserve water while boosting crop yields. With focus on durability and ease of maintenance, we deliver reliable solutions.

Greenhouse Solutions

Modern Controlled Environment Agriculture

We provide high-end greenhouse and screenhouse facilities with galvanized and wooden frames. Our focus is on quality and durability to meet the rising demand due to changing climatic conditions.

Our greenhouse solutions offer cutting-edge climate control, superior ventilation and lighting, enhanced protection against pests and diseases, and year-round crop production capabilities.

Geomembrane Materials

Professional Lining Solutions

Geomembranes/liners are in high demand for water reservoirs in irrigation and fish farming projects. We work with HDPE lining materials, GCL Bentoliner, Geotextile, etc., ensuring quality and durability.

Our expertise comes from working on mining sites, large irrigation projects, and fish farming projects. We install liners at major mining sites and offer repair and construction services.
