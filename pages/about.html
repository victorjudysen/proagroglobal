Our Story

Pro Agro Global Ltd was established with a purpose of supplying general purpose products and materials to major companies, SMEs, and organizations, as well as supply and installation of modern agriculture products and facilities.

In 9 years, the company has already gathered vast experience in its competitive edge to ensure customer satisfaction at the utmost level. We have become a trusted partner in agricultural development across East Africa.

We have a team of highly trained professionals with vast experience to go beyond the sale to ensure that our clients are completely satisfied with our products and services.
