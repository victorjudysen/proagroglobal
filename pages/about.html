About Pro Agro Global

9 years of excellence in modern agriculture solutions across East Africa

Our Story

Pro Agro Global Ltd was established with a clear purpose: to supply general purpose products and materials to major companies, SMEs, and organizations while specializing in the supply and installation of modern agriculture products and facilities.

Over the course of 9 years, our company has gathered vast experience and maintained our competitive edge to ensure customer satisfaction at the utmost level. We have built a reputation as a dynamic and leading company in the distribution of modern agriculture products, facilities, and technologies.

Our team of highly trained professionals possesses vast experience that goes beyond the sale to ensure that our clients are completely satisfied with our products and services. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through quality service and innovative solutions.

Our Mission

To become the world-class supplier of products, facilities as well as technical solutions provider in East Africa, facilitating growth of modern agriculture through innovative solutions and exceptional service delivery.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading provider of modern agricultural solutions in East Africa, empowering farmers and organizations with cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices that drive agricultural transformation.
