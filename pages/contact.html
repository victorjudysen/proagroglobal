Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What services do you provide?

We provide comprehensive agricultural solutions including irrigation systems (drip and sprinkler), greenhouse construction, geomembrane installations (dam and pond liners), and civil engineering services for agricultural infrastructure.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small irrigation systems can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while large greenhouse or infrastructure projects may take 2-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages for all our installations. This includes regular inspections, repairs, system optimization, and 24/7 emergency support. We also provide training for your staff on system operation and basic maintenance.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Tanzania and East Africa, with our main office in Arusha. We have successfully completed projects across multiple regions and are equipped to handle projects nationwide. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can request a quote by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly at +255 782 906 985, or emailing info@proagroglobal.co.tz. We'll schedule a site visit to assess your needs and provide a detailed, customized quotation within 3-5 business days.

Do you provide warranties on your installations?

Yes, all our installations come with comprehensive warranties. Equipment warranties range from 1-5 years depending on the product, and our workmanship is guaranteed for a minimum of 2 years. We use only high-quality, certified materials from reputable manufacturers.
