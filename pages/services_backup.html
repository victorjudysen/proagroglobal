Irrigation Systems

Our expertise and experience in modern irrigation covers both supply and installations of irrigation systems and facilities to SMEs, large corporates, and organizations. We specialize in drip irrigation, sprinkler systems, and automated watering solutions.

  • Drip irrigation systems
  • Sprinkler irrigation
  • Automated control systems
  • System maintenance & support

Greenhouses & Screenhouses

Providing high-end facilities and products for greenhouses and screenhouses of both galvanized and wooden frames. Our obsession is on the quality and durability of our materials and installations.

  • Galvanized frame greenhouses
  • Wooden frame structures
  • Climate control systems
  • Installation & maintenance
Learn More

Geomembranes/Dam Liners

Geomembranes are highly considered for water reservoirs in irrigation projects as well as ponds in fish farming. We work with high-quality HDPE lining materials to ensure both quality and durability.

  • HDPE liner installation
  • Fish pond liners
  • Water reservoir lining
  • Custom liner solutions
